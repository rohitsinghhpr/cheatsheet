Negative Test Cases For Gmail

  • Attempt to log in with an empty email address field.
  • Try logging in with an empty password field.
  • Use an email address that does not have the “@” symbol.
  • Enter a password with fewer than the required characters.
  • Provide an incorrect email format, such as “username@domain.”
  • Use an email address that does not exist.
  • Enter an incorrect password for a valid email address.
  • Try to log in with a deactivated or suspended account.
  • Use special characters in the email address or password.
  • Exceed the maximum character limit for the email address or password.
  • Enter spaces before or after the email address or password.
  • Use different capitalization in the email address (e.g., “example@gmail.com” and “Example@gmail.com”).
  • Attempt to send an email without any recipient.
  • Attach a file with an unsupported format for emails.
  • Attempt to schedule an email for delivery in the past.
